Referral Discount


Returning exhibitors may qualify for up to 10% off their final exhibit booth charge by referring new exhibitors for ABRF 2009. Please submit company name and contact information to abrf@courtesyassoc.com. Be sure to include “New Exhibitor Referral” in the subject line of your email.

Discounts will be applied after the 2009 exhibit and only if the new exhibitor has made full payment. New exhibitors are defined as companies that have not exhibited at an ABRF meeting before.

There is no limit to the number of referral discounts that may be taken per exhibitor. Additional discounts are applied to the new total discount for each additional new referral.
