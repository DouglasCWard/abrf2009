Satellite Workshops and Tours


Satellite workshops and tours will be held right before the start of the ABRF 2009 main meeting:

Satellite Educational Workshops* on genomics and proteomics technologies, including next generation sequencing, HPLC theory and practice, recombinant protein production, proteomics instrumentation and proteome informatics.

Satellite Workshop of the National Network of IDeA-Funded Core Laboratories (NICL), open to all ABRF 2009 attendees who are affiliated with core facilities located in any of the 23 states eligible for IDeA funding.

Tours of core facilities at nearby institutions.

 *Please note that Full Meeting Registration must be purchased to attend a Satellite Educational Workshop.
