Sponsorship Opportunities


ABRF 2009 Main Meeting Sponsorship opportunities are available to exhibiting companies that want to increase their corporate or brand visibility to the ABRF meeting attendees.  All sponsors are acknowledged in the official meeting program, on the ABRF meeting website, on signage throughout the convention center, on booth "thank you" certificates, and on staff ribbons. These sponsorships are offered on an enduring basis to exhibitors.  Current sponsors have the first right-of-refusal.  Companies that are a sponsor of ABRF also receive priority exhibitor booth selection, if they submit their booth application for the following year's meeting at the current year's conference when sales begin for the following year.

Satellite Educational Workshop Sponsorships are now available.  This is a new sponsorship opportunity at ABRF 2009!

Academic Sponsorships for the ABRF 2009 meeting are available for academic institutions and non-profit research institutes.

Media Sponsorship opportunities for the ABRF 2009 meeting are available for publishing companies that want to increase the visibility of their publication media with ABRF meeting attendees. For more information and to apply, please contact Catherine Portner at abrf@courtesyassoc.com, or call +1 (202) 973-8670.

Advertising in the ABRF 2009 Meeting Program Book is available for meeting exhibitors and others.
