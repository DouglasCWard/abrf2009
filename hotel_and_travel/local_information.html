Local Area Information


 

The ABRF 2009 meeting will be held in the Memphis Cook  Convention Center, located downtown in the heart of the city of Memphis, Tennessee.  Nearby Beale Street has world famous music hangouts, back alley barbecue joints and upscale restaurants.  Downtown attractions include Memphis blues clubs and outstanding museums.  The world famous Graceland is in easy travel distance.  For information about places to visit in the Memphis area, please visit www.memphistravel.com.

Some attractions of note in Downtown Memphis:

  • Beale Street Entertainment District
  • Center for Southern Folklore
  • National Civil Rights Museum
  • Memphis Rock 'n Soul Museum
  • St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
  • Slave Haven Underground Railroad Museum
  • Soulsville: Stax Museum of American Soul Music

Parking at the Convention Center

Parking at the Convention Center is available for $5 per entry.  Please note that ABRF 2009 will not validate or offer discounts for parking.
