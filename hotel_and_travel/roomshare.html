Room Sharing


If you are interested in finding a person to share a hotel room, please send your name, telephone/fax number, email address, arrival date, departure date, and your gender to the ABRF Meeting Team, by e-mail at abrf@courtesyassoc.com, or by fax (202) 331-0111.  A list of names and e-mail contact information of attendees interested in sharing a hotel room will be e-mailed to you. You may then contact these individuals.

ABRF assumes no responsibility with respect to this service and accepts no liabilities relative to the service.  Your signature on your cover letter or your e-mail will be your acknowledgment and acceptance of this disclaimer of liability.
