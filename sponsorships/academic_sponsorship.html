Academic Sponsorship Opportunities


 Academic Sponsorship opportunities for the ABRF 2009 meeting are available for academic institutions and non-profit research institutes.  A contribution of $2,000 from an academic institution or non-profit research institute receive the following benefits:

  • One (1) complimentary meeting registration will be provided to each sponsoring academic institution
  • Verbal acknowledgment at the ABRF Annual Meeting
  • Logo of the sponsoring academic institution on the meeting website - https://abrf2009.org
  • Acknowledgment in the ABRF Annual Meeting program
  • Acknowledgment on the signage adverstising the meeting at the convention center during the meeting

Please compelete the ABRF 2009 Annual Meeting Academic Sponsorship Application and mail, fax or e-mail to:

Academic institutions and research institutes can also sponsor the ABRF 2009 meeting through the Satellite Educational Workshop Sponsorship and Meeting Sponsorship opportunities. 

Please inform senior administration officials in your organization about these ABRF meeting sponsorship opportunities and let them know how much your cores benefit from the ABRF and its annual conference.  An ABRF meeting sponsorship can increase the visibility of your academic institution or non-profit research institute in the life sciences core facility community.  Moreover, bringing these sponsorship opportunities to the attention of senior administrators in an academic institution helps spread the word about the important core facility activities of the ABRF and can have a positive impact on the standing of core facilities within an academic organization.

We appreciate your support of the ABRF 2009 conference.
