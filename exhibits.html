Exhibitor Information


You are invited to exhibit at ABRF 2009: Application and Optimization of Existing and Emerging Biotechnologies, at the Memphis Cook Convention Center, February 7-10, 2009, in Memphis, Tennessee.  Come be part of this thirteen year tradition, showcasing the leading experts in life sciences research and biotechnology.

The ABRF meeting is one of the best rated annual gatherings for life sciences researchers and biotechnology laboratories.  Genome Technology readers recently named the ABRF annual meeting as the Most Recommended Meeting for Technology and Instrumentation.

The ABRF would like to thank its exhibitors for joining us for an exciting and productive meeting in Memphis, TN!

If you are interested in purchasing the post-show attendee mailing list, please download this form, complete and fax it to 202-331-0111.

For information on the ABRF 2010 Annual Meeting, please send an e-mail to abrf@courtesyassoc.com or call 202-973-8670.
